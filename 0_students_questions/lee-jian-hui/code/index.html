Global Distributions of Nuclear Reactors

The nuclear reactor frequency is an aggregate sum of all nuclear reactors that a country has. This includes nuclear reactors that are not operational anymore.

It is important to know that not all Countries have Nuclear Power Plants

Historically, United States, France and China has the most numbers of both operational and
non-operational reactors.

Mortality Rate of Each Energy Source

In the color legend, energy types which has mortality rates too low to be represented in the chart is marked as grey colour

The energies with the lowest mortality rates: Hydropower, Nuclear, Solar and Wind

Note that Nuclear Power gets much more hate than what it deserves as it is one of the least harmful energy sources.
